Controls

Must be played with pc

  • up and down arrow keys to move the player up and down
  • Press the space bar to shoot
  • Press the 'd' key to turn on debug mode or to see points for each kill.
  • Try to avoid "samba Hive" as they are hard to kill with low ammo
  • Try to Collide with "Lightbearer Samba" as you can get powered up with ammo.
